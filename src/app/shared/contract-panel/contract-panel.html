ANNEXURE A: SERVICE LEVEL AGREEMENT (SLA)
Uptime Commitments, Support Levels, & Escalation Procedures
1. Service Availability & Uptime Target
1.1. Unitecare guarantees a Monthly Uptime Percentage of 99.9% for the core Contracts and KYC Verification services.
1.2. Uptime is monitored 24/7 by external monitoring agents, and is defined as the platform being fully accessible and functioning for end-users.
1.3. Scheduled Maintenance Windows shall occur between 02:00 AM and 04:00 AM UAE Standard Time on weekends and do not count as downtime, provided a 48-hour prior notification is sent to the Client.
2. Priority Support & Response Targets
Severity Level Definition Initial Response Target Resolution
Priority 1 (Critical) Core services unavailable, patient record lookup or document signature blocked globally. < 30 Minutes 4 Hours
Priority 2 (High) System active but experiencing degraded performance; form builder or review loading very slow. < 2 Hours 12 Hours
Priority 3 (Normal) Minor UI updates, layout suggestions, non-blocking bug reports, or general account inquiries. < 8 Hours 48 Hours
3. Service Credits & Remedies
3.1. If availability falls below the 99.9% threshold in a given calendar month, Client is eligible to claim Service Credits as follows:
  • Uptime 99.0% - 99.8%: 10% credit of monthly subscription fee.
  • Uptime 95.0% - 98.9%: 25% credit of monthly subscription fee.
  • Uptime < 95.0%: 50% credit of monthly subscription fee.

ANNEXURE B: REGULATORY & DATA PRIVACY ADDENDUM
UAE Federal Healthcare Laws, DHA Compliance & Patient Privacy Standards
1. Local Data Residency Commitments
1.1. In accordance with UAE Federal Law No. 2 of 2019 (concerning the use of Information and Communication Technology in Health Fields) and DHA guidelines, all patient health information, trade licenses, DHA medical practice permits, and KYC materials must be hosted on local secure servers located strictly within the territory of the United Arab Emirates.
1.2. Unitecare confirms that its production hosting providers are ISO/IEC 27001 certified and fully compliant with DESC (Dubai Electronic Security Center) regulations.
2. Healthcare Professional License (DHA) Tracking
2.1. The Client agrees to immediately submit and update all licenses of active medical practitioners (DHA licenses) inside the KYC module of the platform upon any changes or renewals.
2.2. The platform will send automated expiration notifications 90 days, 60 days, and 30 days prior to license expirations to ensure compliance with DHA clinic licensing requirements.
3. Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery
3.1. Unitecare maintains continuous database replication with full recovery point objective (RPO) of < 5 minutes and recovery time objective (RTO) of < 1 hour.
